# pre-mortem Skill Evaluation

## Test Case 1: Product Launch Risk Analysis
**Input:** "Pre-mortem for enterprise dashboard launch in 6 weeks"
**Expected Output:**
- Failure scenarios across GTM, product, market
- Likelihood and impact scoring
- Mitigation strategies per risk
- Go/no-go recommendation
- Monitoring plan

## Test Case 2: Pricing Change Pre-Mortem
**Input:** "We're raising prices 40% next quarter. What could go wrong?"
**Expected Output:**
- Customer churn risk analysis
- Competitive response scenarios
- Internal resistance patterns
- Communication strategy risks
- Rollback plan

## Test Case 3: New Market Entry
**Input:** "Pre-mortem: entering healthcare vertical for first time"
**Expected Output:**
- Market-specific risks (regulatory, buying patterns)
- Competitive positioning risks
- GTM execution risks
- Mitigation roadmap
- Success metrics with early warning indicators
