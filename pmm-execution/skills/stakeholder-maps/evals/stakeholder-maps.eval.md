# stakeholder-maps Skill Evaluation

## Test Case 1: Build from Scratch
**Input:** "Build stakeholder map for Q4 enterprise product launch"
**Expected Output:**
- Stakeholder grid with power/interest axes
- Clear champion/blocker/neutral classifications
- Engagement strategy per stakeholder
- RACI matrix for launch activities

## Test Case 2: Update Existing Map
**Input:** "Our CTO just became a blocker on the pricing change. Update the map."
**Expected Output:**
- Updated power/interest positioning
- Root cause analysis of the shift
- Mitigation strategies
- Communication plan adjustment

## Test Case 3: Cross-Functional Alignment
**Input:** "I need to align Sales, CS, and Product on this launch. Who do I brief first?"
**Expected Output:**
- Sequencing strategy based on influence patterns
- Briefing content tailored per stakeholder
- Pre-emption of objections
- Coalition-building recommendations
