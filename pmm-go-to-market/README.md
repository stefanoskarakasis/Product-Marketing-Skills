# PMM-Go-to-Market

- **go-to-market-strategy** — Go-to-market strategy and launch execution skills for B2B SaaS Product Marketing Managers.

## Skills (2)

### go-to-market-strategy
Brain-powered GTM strategy generator. Assigns launch tier (T1-T4), generates strategic brief with messaging, channels, metrics, and timeline. Self-learning via brain Section 7 memory.

### workflow-orchestrator
Multi-skill PMM workflow orchestrator. Chains 20+ hs- skills for complete GTM programs. Covers 10 workflows: full launch, positioning refresh, competitive program, quarterly PMM cycle, market entry, post-launch retro, competitive response, ICP foundation, voice foundation, and new hire onboarding. Reads brain context, writes back to brain, produces master program documents.

**Triggers:** 'run full GTM workflow', 'launch [product]', 'refresh positioning', 'quarterly PMM cycle', 'competitive program'

**Orchestrates:** All hs- skills (positioning, competitive, execution, research) + pmm-toolkit skills

**Brain Integration:** Reads Sections 1-7, writes to Sections 3, 4, 5, 7

**Read more:** [workflow-orchestrator/](workflow-orchestrator/)
