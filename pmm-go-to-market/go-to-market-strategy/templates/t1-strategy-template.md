### Week 0: Launch Day

**Morning (8am-12pm):**

- [ ] Blog post published (9am)
- [ ] Landing page/website live (9am)
- [ ] Customer email sent (10am — stagger by segment)
- [ ] In-product messaging activates (10am)

- [ ] Sales/CS begin outreach to target accounts
- [ ] CEO/leadership social posts (personal LinkedIn, X)

**Afternoon (12pm-5pm):**
- [ ] Monitor media coverage and social sentiment
- [ ] Respond to comments/questions on social
- [ ] Track early adoption metrics (activations, signups, demo requests)
- [ ] Triage any critical bugs or issues
- [ ] Internal Slack updates (metrics, coverage, customer reactions)

**Owners:** PMM (conductor), Marketing (execution), Sales/CS (outreach), Support (monitoring), Product (bug triage)

---

### Week +1 to +4: Post-Launch

**Week 1:**
- [ ] Webinar held (day 3-5 of launch week)
- [ ] Follow-up emails sent (to webinar attendees, non-openers)
- [ ] Monitor adoption curve and flag any drop-offs
- [ ] Customer feedback collection begins (NPS survey, in-app prompt)

**Week 2:**
- [ ] Paid campaigns optimized (pause low performers, scale winners)
- [ ] Second round of PR outreach (media that didn't pick up initially)
- [ ] Sales feedback synthesis (what's landing, what's not)

**Week 3-4:**
- [ ] Bug fixes prioritized and shipped
- [ ] Metrics review (T+30 leading indicators)
- [ ] Content repurposing (webinar clips, customer quotes, blog posts)

**Week 4:**
- [ ] Post-launch retrospective (T+30 review)
- [ ] Update brain Section 7 with lessons learned

**Owners:** PMM (coordination), Demand Gen (optimization), Product (bugs), CS (feedback)
