# Success Metrics Defaults by Tier

## T1 Metrics (90-day target)

**Adoption:**
- 30%+ of target segment activated
- 20%+ feature usage after 90 days

**Revenue:**
- $X new pipeline (defined per launch)
- $X new bookings/ARR (if applicable)
- Win rate vs. alternatives: +5-10%

**Engagement:**
- Feature adoption curve (expected S-curve by week 8)
- Support ticket volume (reduction if improvement)
- Customer satisfaction (NPS, CSAT)

**Market:**
- Share of voice (earned media mentions)
- Analyst coverage
- Sales win rate vs. competitors

---

## T2 Metrics (60-day target)

**Adoption:**
- 20%+ of target segment activated
- 15%+ feature usage

**Revenue:**
- $X new pipeline (smaller than T1)
- Win rate improvement: +3-5%

**Engagement:**
- Feature adoption trajectory
- Customer feedback loop closed

---

## T3 Metrics (30-day target)

**Adoption:**
- 10%+ adoption within 30 days
- Positive customer feedback

**Revenue:**
- Usage metrics (if applicable)

**Support:**
- Ticket volume/sentiment
- Customer satisfaction

---

## T4 Metrics (Immediate)

**Adoption:**
- 100% internal awareness
- Any measurable reduction in support tickets

**Engagement:**
- Feature telemetry (if tracked)
