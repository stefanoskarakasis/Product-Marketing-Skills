# GTM Tactics by Tier

## T1 Tactics Checklist

- [ ] Competitive positioning deck (vs. top 3 alternatives)
- [ ] 3+ customer case studies or testimonials
- [ ] Press release + media list
- [ ] Analyst briefing (Gartner/Forrester)
- [ ] Sales training + battlecard
- [ ] Customer success playbook
- [ ] Webinar + follow-up sequence
- [ ] Landing page + paid ads
- [ ] Executive talking points
- [ ] Partner/channel enablement

**Owned by:** PM + PMM (joint)

---

## T2 Tactics Checklist

- [ ] Sales one-pager
- [ ] Competitive comparison
- [ ] Customer quote/testimonial
- [ ] Email announcement (2-3 send)
- [ ] Webinar or lunch & learn
- [ ] Analyst briefing (if relevant)
- [ ] Release notes + in-app messaging
- [ ] Slack/community post

**Owned by:** PMM

---

## T3 Tactics Checklist

- [ ] Release notes (clear, benefit-focused)
- [ ] Email announcement (single send)
- [ ] In-app notification
- [ ] Sales email template

**Owned by:** Product

---

## T4 Tactics Checklist

- [ ] Changelog entry
- [ ] Slack message

**Owned by:** Product (self-serve)
