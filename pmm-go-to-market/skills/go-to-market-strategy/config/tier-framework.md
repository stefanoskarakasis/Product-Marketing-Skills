# GTM Tier Framework

## T1: Company Bet (6-12 weeks, High budget)

**When to use:**
- Major product launch
- New market entry (vertical/geography)
- Significant pricing change
- Merger/acquisition announcement
- Platform shift

**GTM investment:** Full cross-functional campaign
- Dedicated PM/PMM
- Sales enablement + training
- Customer success playbooks
- Press/analyst engagement
- Event/webinar series
- Paid advertising budget

**Success metrics (typical):**
- 30%+ adoption within 90 days
- $X pipeline impact
- X% feature adoption
- Market share lift
- Customer satisfaction scores

**Timeline:** 6-12 weeks pre-launch + 4-week execution

---

## T2: Major Initiative (2-4 weeks, Medium budget)

**When to use:**
- Significant feature launch (new market)
- Expansion to new customer segment
- Major competitive response
- Key partnership announcement

**GTM investment:** Focused execution
- 1-2 week campaign
- Sales deck + objection handling
- Customer communication
- Analyst briefings
- Webinar or lunch & learn

**Success metrics (typical):**
- 20%+ adoption within 60 days
- $X pipeline
- Customer feedback loop closed

**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks pre-launch + 2-week execution

---

## T3: Routine Launch (1 week, Standard budget)

**When to use:**
- Regular feature for existing customers
- Minor expansion
- Performance improvement
- Bug fix with customer impact

**GTM investment:** Light-touch
- Release notes
- Email announcement
- Product in-app messaging
- Sales one-pager

**Success metrics (typical):**
- 10%+ adoption within 30 days
- Customer support ticket tracking

**Timeline:** 1 week pre-launch + 1 week execution

---

## T4: Minimal Lift (<1 day, Low budget)

**When to use:**
- Internal-only feature
- Minor UI improvement
- Documentation update
- Small bug fix

**GTM investment:** Minimal
- Changelog update
- Slack announcement
- Product release notes

**Success metrics (typical):**
- Internal awareness
- Support ticket reduction

**Timeline:** <1 day
